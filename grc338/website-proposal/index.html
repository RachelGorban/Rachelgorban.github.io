Introduction

Ray's Coffee Co.

Ray's Coffee Co. is a place for artists and community memebers alike to gather, commune, and enjoy the wonderous things that come out of sharing a cup of coffee.

Target audience

The people using this site are local community members, people passing through town looking to have a sip of coffee at the best coffee shop in town, and all who are looking for a safe space to explore. The target audience is students, families, artists, musicians, and open-minded people looking to connect.

Customers will come to this site to look at upcoming events, monthly specialty orders, and local artist and musician showcases. People who are not regular customers will use the site to look at the regular menu, location, and find out more about us.
